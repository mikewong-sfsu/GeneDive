Search Modes

1-Hop

Provide one DGD to view all interactions with that DGD. Provide one DGD to view all direct interactions with the DGD.

2-Hop

Provide two DGDs as input. GeneDive will show interactions between the two DGDs through up to 1 (one) intermediary DGD.

3-Hop

Provide two DGDs as input. GeneDive will show interactions between the two DGDs through up to 2 (two) intermediary DGDs.

Clique

Provide one DGD as input. GeneDive will show those DchiGDs that interact with input DGD and one of its interactants.

Using the Graph

Navigate

Click and drag on whitespace to move around.

Manipulate

Click and drag genes to move them around. Genes will stay where you leave them.

Shift-Click Gene to Add

Shift-Click on genes to add them to the search. Releasing shift will start a new search.

Ctrl-Click Gene to Start New Search

Ctrl-Click on a gene to start a new search with only that gene.
